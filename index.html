Welcome To Our Vintage Clocks Shop!

Vintage Clocks Shop is a result of deep passion for vintage design combined with the fascination by the concept of time, the way we imagine & perceive time.

After many years of dreaming about owning a unique store, in 2011 me and my sister bought a property, renovated it and started a business from scratch. Ten years later, with much dedication and hard work we can proudly say that our shop is one of the most visited shops in the city.

Located in the heart of Stockholm, our vintage shop provides you with a huge selection of the most authentic retro clocks. We have them in various shapes, colors and sizes.

We are open everyday, 08:00 - 20:00.
